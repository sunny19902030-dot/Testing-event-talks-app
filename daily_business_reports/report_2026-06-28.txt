======================================================================
* Entity: Federal Reserve System (Macroeconomic Policy)
* Headline: "America In Focus: Key inflation gauge surges to 3-year high, mortgage rate climbs - AP News"
* Business Model:
  - Central Banking / Monetary Authority: Regulates money supply, manages interest rates, and controls the availability of credit to stabilize prices and employment.
* Key Economics:
  - Interest Rate Transmission: Raising the federal funds rate increases borrow costs across credit markets (mortgages, credit cards), reducing demand and slowing economic activity.
